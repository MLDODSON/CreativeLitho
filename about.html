About Us

Established in 1979, Creative Litho, Inc. is a locally owned and family operated full service printing and copy shop. We are a union shop located in St. Louis's historic Cherokee Street business district. Creative Litho offers a variety of products and services at everyday low prices ranging from printing, copying, graphic design and bindery. Whether you're an individual, business, group, organization or politician, Creative Litho's services are tailored to meet your needs. Our experienced staff is here to assist you.
